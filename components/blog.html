  • Know at least one, preferably two, n*log(n) sorting algorithm.

Hashtables:

Trees:

  • Know tree construction, transveral, and manuplation algorithms.
  • Be familar with binary trees, n-ary trees, and trie-trees
  • Know how to implement at least one balanced tree: red/black tree, splay tree or AVL tree.
  • Know tree transversal algorithms: BFS and DFS.
  • Know the difference between inorder, postorder and preorder.

Graphs:

  • Know objects representation of a graph (Pros & Cons)
  • Know pointers representation of a graph (Pros & Cons)
  • Know adjacency list representation of a graph (Pros & Cons)
  • Know graph transversal algorithms: breath-first search and depth-first search.
  • Know computational complexity, pros and cons, and implementation of those algorithms.
